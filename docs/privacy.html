Privacy Policy

Hadith Reader · Last updated 2026

Hadith Reader is built to respect your privacy.

What we collect

Nothing. The entire hadith library is bundled inside the app and read on-device — the app does not require a network connection to function and does not upload any personal data. Your bookmarks are stored only on your device.

Subscriptions

Purchases are processed by Apple via StoreKit. We never see your name, email, or payment details — Apple handles all of that.

Analytics & tracking

The app does not track you across apps or websites and does not use advertising identifiers.
