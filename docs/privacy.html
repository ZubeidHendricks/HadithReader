Privacy Policy

Hadith Reader · Last updated 2026

Hadith Reader ("the app") is built to respect your privacy. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

What we collect

Your documents and scans never leave your device. Scanning, OCR (text recognition), and PDF generation all run entirely on-device. We do not upload, store, or have access to your documents or the text within them.

Subscriptions

Purchases are processed by Apple and managed through our subscription provider, RevenueCat, to validate your subscription status. This involves a non-personal app user identifier and your purchase/subscription status. We do not receive your name, email, or payment details — those are handled by Apple.

Permissions

  • Camera — used only to read hadith. Images are processed on-device.
  • Photo Library — used only to import images you choose, and to save scans you export.

Analytics & tracking

The app does not track you across other apps or websites and does not use advertising identifiers.
